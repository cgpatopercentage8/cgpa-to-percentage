How to Convert CGPA to Percentage Easily

Converting your CGPA (Cumulative Grade Point Average) into a percentage can be confusing for many students. CGPA is a grading system commonly used in schools, colleges, and universities worldwide. However, many job applications, universities, and exam boards may require your score in percentage format. That’s why knowing how to convert CGPA to percentage is essential.

What is CGPA?

CGPA, or Cumulative Grade Point Average, represents a student’s overall academic achievement. It’s a numerical value calculated based on grades received in different subjects. CGPA is generally calculated on a scale of 10 in Indian universities and schools.

Why Convert CGPA to Percentage?

Some organizations and academic institutions may require your scores in percentage form. Converting CGPA to percentage gives a clearer understanding of where you stand academically, especially when applying for jobs, scholarships, or further studies.

How to Convert CGPA to Percentage?

The most common formula used is:

Percentage = CGPA × 9.5

For example, if your CGPA is 8.0, then your percentage is calculated as:

Percentage = 8.0 × 9.5 = 76%
